# Year View Templates

![year_view_templates](/img/year_view_templates.png)

- [event_class](api/template/event_class.md) - the CSS style for the event container
- [month_day](api/template/month_day.md) - the format of the day in a cell
- [year_date](api/template/year_date.md) - the date in the header of the view
- [year_month](api/template/year_month.md) - the name of a month in the header of a month block of the view
- [year_scale_date](api/template/year_scale_date.md) - the name of a day in the sub-header of a month block of the view
- [year_tooltip](api/template/year_tooltip.md) - the tooltip over a day cell containing some scheduled event(s)
