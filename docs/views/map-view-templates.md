# Map View Templates

![map_view_templates](/img/map_view_templates.png)

- [map_date](api/template/map_date.md) - the date in the header of the view
- [map_text](api/template/map_text.md) - the text in the second column of the view
- [map_time](api/template/map_time.md) - the date in the first column of the view
- [map_info_content](api/template/map_info_content.md) - the content of the info window in the Map View
