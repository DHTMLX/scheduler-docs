# Mini Calendar Templates

![mini_calendar_templates](/img/mini_calendar_templates.png)

- [calendar_date](api/template/calendar_date.md) - the format of the day in a cell
- [calendar_month](api/template/calendar_month.md) - the date in the header of the calendar
- [calendar_scale_date](api/template/calendar_scale_date.md) - the name of the day in the week sub-header of the view
- [calendar_time](api/template/calendar_time.md) - the date format of the lightbox start and end date inputs
